Emerald
Emerald is a devoted Beagle mix that was given to us by a Florida rescue partner. Two of this unique girl's greatest loves in the world are squeaky tennis balls and her adored, dependable handlers. Emerald is seeking a home with an adoptive family with expertise who will commit the necessary time and effort to fostering a trustworthy relationship. Emerald would love to live as the sole pet in a single-person suburban home with a sizable fenced-in backyard where she could spend endless hours chasing those squeaking tennis balls. Shadow
Shy, lovely Shadow relied on us to help her find a new home after losing the only one she had ever known. After adjusting to her new circumstances, she had the reputation of being a little shadow who enjoyed spending time with her foster family. If you give this 15-year-old healthy pet the stability of a loving home—ideally as the only pet—you'll find it easy to win her over. Do you want to be Shadow's special someone? Ask to speak with her right now!
Adopt Shadow Today!
Sinatra
Prepare to create some lovely music with Frank Sinatra! This Tennessee-born little Hound mix is quite endearing. Sinatra is a vibrant individual that enjoys trying new things and would enjoy living in a busy home. For Sinatra, the ideal house would be one in which he is the sole pet and the center of attention.
